## Width Question in case of border box and content box 

~~~

In case of content box : 
ex -
 width:200px
 border: 10px solid blue ; 
 padding : 20px;

 then the whole width will be : 
 200px(content size) + border 20 px (border-left , border-right) + padding 40px(padding-left , padding-right) 
 = 260px

 whereas in case of border box : 
 border 20 px (border-left , border-right) + padding 40px(padding-left , padding-right) + 140px(content-size) = 200px
 
~~~
