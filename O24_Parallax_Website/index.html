Getting around

Top Destinations

If you're looking for inspiration for your next trip, here are a few of our top destinations for 2023: Iceland: Iceland is a land of otherworldly landscapes, with glaciers, volcanoes, waterfalls, and black sand beaches. It's also a great place to see the Northern Lights. Japan: Japan is a country with a rich culture and history, as well as stunning scenery. Visitors can enjoy everything from ancient temples and shrines to modern cities and delicious food. Peru: Peru is home to the world-famous Machu Picchu, but there's much more to this country than just one attraction. Visitors can also explore ancient ruins, hike through the Amazon rainforest, and visit floating islands on Lake Titicaca. Croatia: Croatia is a hidden gem in Europe, with stunning coastline, picturesque villages, and national parks. It's a great place to relax and enjoy the Mediterranean lifestyle. South Africa: South Africa is a country with something to offer everyone, from wildlife safaris to cosmopolitan cities and beautiful beaches.

Staying safe

Travel Tips

Here are a few travel tips to help you plan your next trip: Be flexible with your travel dates. If you can travel during the off-season, you'll often find better deals on flights and accommodation. Book your flights in advance. The earlier you book, the more likely you are to get a good deal. Pack light. You'll be glad you did when you're lugging your suitcase around airports and train stations. Bring a travel adapter. If you're traveling to a different country, make sure to bring a travel adapter so you can charge your electronics. Learn a few basic phrases in the local language. This will show that you're making an effort and help you get around more easily.
